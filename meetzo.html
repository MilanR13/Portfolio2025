MEETZO

An app designed to connect people through shared hobbies and
help them meet up to practice them together

MY ROLE

I joined the project as the sole designer, with the goal of giving the app a visual refresh while drawing more attention to its core feature, connecting people through shared hobbies. The redesign also aimed to improve the overall experience by making it easier to switch between different content views without confusing them with filters or categories.
The main challenges I was faced with were:

  • Redesigning the existing app within the constraints of the current UX

  • Consolidating two separate app views into a cohesive experience
