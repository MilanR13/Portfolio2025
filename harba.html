HARBA

a mobile platform designed to connect boat owners with
marine professionals through a task bidding system

MY ROLE

This was a design test task completed in 12 hours, where I was asked to explore the experience of boat owners posting repair or maintenance jobs and receiving offers from marine craftsmen. The focus was on crafting an intuitive, well-structured UX with clear task states and category separation.
The main phases of my work included: Mapping use cases, defining task flows, designing UI screens, and developing a visual system.
